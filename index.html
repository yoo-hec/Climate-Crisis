Hawaii's Paradise is Under Threat!

Hawaii has been going through negavtive impact due to climate change. A lot of this issues are greatly affecting both the natural environment and wildlife living in Hawaii. Learn more in the facts below

6 Critical Climate Facts About Hawaii

1

Rising Sea Levels

Hawaii is experiencing sea level rise at approximately 1.5 inches per decade, threatening coastal areas and iconic beaches.

2

Coral Reef Bleaching

Over 50% of Hawaii's coral reefs experienced bleaching events between 2014 and 2017 due to rising ocean temperatures.

3

Changing Rainfall Patterns

Hawaii has seen a 25% decrease in rainfall over the past 30 years, affecting agriculture and freshwater availability.

4

Endangered Species

Hawaii has over 500 endangered species - more than any other U.S. state - with climate change accelerating habitat loss.

5

Stronger Storms

The intensity of tropical storms affecting Hawaii has increased by 15% since 1980, with more severe hurricanes predicted.

6

Increase of Wildfires

Climate change over the years has made Hawaii prone to more wildfires due to the incease in temperature and dryer areas.
